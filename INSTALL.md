### For iPhone/iPad Users (Safari)

1. **Open the app** in Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap the **Share button** (the square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if you want, then tap **"Add"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen
6. Tap the icon to open Health AI as a full-screen app!

> **Note:** On iOS, you must use Safari browser for installation. Chrome and other browsers don't support PWA installation on iOS.
